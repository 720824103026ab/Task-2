📈 DASHBOARD CALCULATIONS (What Each KPI Shows)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Total Sales = SUM of all "Sales" column values
Total Revenue = SUM of all "Revenue" column values
Total Orders = COUNT of transactions
Avg Order Value = Total Revenue ÷ Total Orders
